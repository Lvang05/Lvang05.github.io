Cancun, Mexico

Cancun Mexico is the ultimate vacation and resort destination whether your idea of a good time is sunbathing, scuba, adventure, eco-parks, nighlife, water sports among many other activities. For divers and snorkelers, Cancun is a paradise. The crystal-blue Caribbean waters hold an abundance of underwater wildlife, and most of the resorts have trainers who can show you which end of the fin to flap and how to handle the equipment. Want to taste the Caribbean nightlife? The hotel zone in Cancun is your hot spot, with numerous world-famous bars and nightclubs that drive the night to the beat of everything from salsa to club mixes and world sounds.

Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

Punta Cana is a popular resort region on the eastern coast of the Dominican Republic, known for its beautiful beaches, all-inclusive resorts, and water sports like snorkeling, kitesurfing, and sailing. It features several attractions including Saona Island, the Indigenous Eyes Ecological Park, and world-class golf courses. The region is served by the Punta Cana International Airport (PUJ), a modern facility within the Puntacana Resort & Club.
