Community-Centered News Agency Statement

At Leon's news, we believe that trusted journalism begins and ends with the community. As a dedicated local news agency, our mission is to deliver accurate, timely, and relevant information that reflects the voices, stories, and values of the people we serve. Our newsroom is not just a place where stories are written — it's where the community comes together. Whether we're covering city council meetings, spotlighting local heroes, or addressing pressing issues like education, safety, or economic development, we put our readers at the heart of every headline. We work closely with schools, neighborhood organizations, cultural institutions, and small businesses to ensure inclusive coverage that empowers and informs. We also welcome contributions and feedback from residents, because we believe journalism should be a two-way conversation — not just a broadcast. At our core, we are more than a news outlet — we are a bridge between people, a platform for dialogue, and a trusted companion in community growth.

Leon Vang
