About Us

Founded in 2010, Leon's Real Estate Group began as a small family-run agency with a big vision: to bring honesty, integrity, and personalized service to the local housing market. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in real estate, helping hundreds of families find their perfect single-family homes. Built on a foundation of strong community relationships and a deep understanding of the neighborhoods we serve, Leon's Real Estate Group is proud to be a locally owned and operated business that puts people first.

We specialize exclusively in residential single-family homes, offering expert support whether you're buying your first home, upgrading to a larger space, or relocating. Our team of experienced agents combines market insight with a friendly, no-pressure approach to help you make confident decisions. With a strong commitment to transparency, communication, and customer satisfaction, Leon's Real Estate Group continues to set the standard for exceptional service in the real estate industry.
